# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 11 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Agora: The Flashcard Exchange ("Agora", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

---

## Information we collect

### Account information
When you create an account, we collect your **email address** and **display name**. If you sign in with Google, we receive these from your Google account. We do not collect your password directly — authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication.

### App content
Cards, card sets, templates, and study session data you create are stored in your account on Google Cloud Firestore. This data is associated with your user ID and is only accessible to you.

### Crash and diagnostic data
If the app crashes or encounters an error, we collect diagnostic information through Firebase Crashlytics. This may include device model, operating system version, app version, and the state of the app at the time of the error. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

### Feedback and reports
If you choose to submit a feedback report or issue through the in-app Send Report feature, we collect the content of your report, your display name, your email address, and optionally a snapshot of recent app log activity. Submitting a report is entirely voluntary.

---

## How we use your information

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Email address, display name | Authentication; identifying your account |
| App content (cards, sets, sessions) | Providing and syncing the core app functionality |
| Crash data | Diagnosing and fixing bugs |
| Feedback reports | Improving the app; investigating reported issues |

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy.

---

## Third-party services

Agora uses the following Google/Firebase services, each governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy):

- **Firebase Authentication** — manages sign-in
- **Cloud Firestore** — stores your app content
- **Firebase Storage** — stores card images and audio
- **Firebase Crashlytics** — collects crash reports
- **Google Sign-In** — optional sign-in method

---

## Data retention and deletion

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the **Account & Settings** screen in the app. Crash reports are retained for 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics.

---

## Children's privacy

Agora is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

---

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
